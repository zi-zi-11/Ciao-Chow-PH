Here With You: Supporting Communities and Giving Back at Ciao, Chow


Our services don’t stop within the confines of our restaurants. At Ciao, Chow, we believe that true impact happens when businesses actively engage with the communities they serve. And we don’t just serve meals; we serve communities, proving that giving back is the most satisfying flavor of all. For us, giving back is more than a responsibility— it is our way of upholding the values that the founders instilled in Ciao, Chow from the very beginning. Community Service, Accessibility, and Innovation are at the heart of everything that we do. Our core values shape the way we operate and how we interact with our customers and local communities.


We honor our partnership with the Mother Earth Foundation, a non-government organization focused on tackling pressing environmental issues such as waste and toxic pollution, climate change, and other health and environmental justice issues. Their advocacy for zero-waste practices aligns perfectly with our own efforts to reduce our environmental footprint. We proudly observe these practices by using eco-friendly packaging and offering a special promotion for customers who bring their own utensils, encouraging sustainable dining habits. Our goal is to make every meal a step toward a cleaner, more sustainable world.


In addition to our partnership, we also host and sponsor our own charity events in the form of ‘fun’-raisers. Participating in these events can range from simply dining in our restaurant to engaging in activities designed to raise awareness and funds for important causes. These events are our way of bringing together customers, employees, and local organizations to create a positive impact on the world around us.


Every bite you take helps support our ongoing community initiatives. By choosing to dine with us, not only will you be enjoying a delicious meal, you’re also contributing to a greater cause. Each visit contributes to the collective effort of making a positive impact on the environment, local communities, and beyond. At Ciao, Chow, we believe that the simple act of sharing a meal can make a meaningful difference, and with your support, we continue to work toward a better, more sustainable future.
